A list of contacts is a way to win news leads and open new opportunities. For this, it is very important to maintain a list of up-to-date contacts.

In Contacts you will find the id, name and email of the person who makes the decision, the phone number and the name of the company.

Type:
1 - To access a contact specific
2 - To access all the contacts

Type BACK to back the menu
Type EXIT to exit the CRM
